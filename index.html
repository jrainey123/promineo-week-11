Tic Tac Toe



Instructions

Player names are PlayerX and PlayerO. When the cursor hovers over a cell the marker will indicate whose turn it is, X or O. PlayerX goes first. The first player to win a row is the winner. If all cells of the grid are filled with no matching rows then the game is a draw, no winner. Rows are across (3 rows), down (3 rows), diagonal (2 rows).
